About Us

A distribution partner built on reliability

YK Group of Companies is a trusted distribution, import & export, clearing & forwarding, and logistics company. We specialise in beverages, FMCG products, and industrial trading, while delivering dependable supply chain solutions from origin to shelf.

From bottled water to biscuits, baby care to industrial goods, our network exists to make sure the right product reaches the right business at the right time, every time.
